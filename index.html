About me

Finance professional with a process-first mindset

I am a finance and accounts professional based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, with over 6 years of hands-on experience in Record to Report (R2R), accounts payable, bank and payment gateway reconciliation, GST compliance, and MIS reporting.

Currently at Ebix Travels Pvt. Ltd. (Via.com), I manage multi-gateway payment reconciliations across TPSL, PayU, CCAvenue, HDFC, and ICICI, process airline backend refunds, and support month-end closing — handling high volumes with a focus on accuracy and zero-error compliance.

Previously at Kautilya Corporate Solutions, I led GST filings, ledger posting, and ROC compliance (AOC-4, MGT-7, DIR-3 KYC). My foundation in audit began at A.K. Sinha & Co., where I supported school and bank audits.

I hold an M.Com from IGNOU and a BBM from Mirza Ghalib College, along with a Tax Pro Professional certification covering advanced accounting, direct taxation, and GST from ITAA Education.
